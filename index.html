Who We Are

Lunnovate is a youth-led innovation network committed to bridging the gap between young Africans and emerging technologies. Rooted in Nigeria, we equip students with the tools and mindset to not just use technology but create it.

Our Vision

A future where every young African can confidently shape and lead in the digital world, becoming producers, not just consumers.

Our Mission

To localize tech education, foster innovation, and inspire leadership through relatable, accessible, and impactful learning experiences.

Core Objectives

Early exposure to AI and digital tools
Promote Tech-powered problem-solving
Integrate Indigenous language learning
Establish School-based innovation communities

Key Programs

#MakeTechNative Movement

Our flagship initiative: Tech workshops that bring AI and digital skills to secondary schools in culturally relatable ways.

AI Awareness Workshops

Hands-on sessions teaching students the core mechanics of AI and positioning them as active participants in its future development.

EcoBridge (Hausa AI Chatbot)

Developing AI-powered tools that facilitate learning and knowledge access in native languages, starting with Hausa.

Impact & Vision

Milestones Achieved

  • Successfully hosted workshops at schools in Kano.
  • Trained 1000+ of students in AI basics and critical digital skills.
  • Received overwhelmingly positive feedback from teachers and students alike.

Future Goals

  • Scale the #MakeTechNative Movement nationwide.

  • Expand the EcoBridge project to include more native African languages.
